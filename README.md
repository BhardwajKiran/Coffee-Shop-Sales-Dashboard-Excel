# Coffee-Shop-Sales-Dashboard-Excel

## 📌 Project Overview

The Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard is an interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, revenue trends, customer purchasing patterns, and profitability across multiple cities and product categories.

This dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights through dynamic visualizations, KPIs, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Slicers, enabling users to monitor performance and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Track overall sales and revenue performance.
* Analyze transactions across different years and months.
* Identify top-performing product categories.
* Compare revenue and profit trends over time.
* Evaluate city-wise sales contribution.
* Provide an interactive reporting experience using Excel.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Transactions
* Total Quantity Sold
* Total Revenue
* Total Profit

### Interactive Filters

* Year Filter (2021, 2022, 2023)
* Month Filter
* City Filter

### Visualizations

#### 📈 Revenue by Category

Analyze revenue generated by:

* Coffee Beverages
* Tea Beverages
* Specialty Drinks
* Snacks
* Sandwiches
* Desserts
* Cold Drinks

#### 💰 Profit by Category

Compare profitability across product categories to identify high-margin products.

#### 📅 Transactions by Weekday

Track customer activity and transaction volume throughout the week.

#### 🥧 Revenue by Year

Year-over-year revenue contribution analysis using a donut chart.

#### 📊 Transactions by Month

Monthly transaction trends comparison across multiple years.

#### 🌍 Revenue % by City

Analyze revenue distribution across:

* Austin
* Chicago
* Los Angeles
* New York
* Seattle

#### 📉 Revenue & Profit by Month

Monitor monthly revenue and profit performance to identify seasonal trends.

## 📈 Key Insights

* Chicago and New York contribute a significant share of total revenue.
* Desserts and Tea Beverages are among the highest revenue-generating categories.
* Transaction volume peaks on Fridays.
* Revenue remains relatively stable throughout the year with slight seasonal variations.
* Profit trends closely follow revenue performance, indicating consistent margins.
